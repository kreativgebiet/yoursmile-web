Do Great Things with Your Smile

What Can You Do with Your Smile?

At YSCTW, you make donations and support a wide range of charitable projects using just your smile. Simply upload a selfie or any other picture of your choice and select one or several projects to which you would like to make a donation. What’s more, the full amount of every donation you make goes directly to the projects you have selected.

Projects and Vision

Our projects focus on the long-term and sustainable development of vital infrastructure in countries or crisis-hit areas in need of aid. Your donations will help to build schools, farms, and hospitals, creating jobs and ensuring the supply of essential, life-saving resources. Help provide a secure future for an entire population with your smile.

Your Download

By downloading our app, you allow us to fund and implement a range of charitable projects. The full amount of your donations goes to the projects you have selected – we use the app download fee to cover our own costs.

Your Donation

You can use our app to turn a selfie into something truly valuable and worthwhile. Here at YSCTW, we have recognized and harnessed the power of the selfie: the greatest invention of this century! Because we are not only changing the world with our app – we are showing the world the true value and power of a smile.

Your Help

You are already doing great things with your donations. But it is also important to tell your friends and family about what you are doing. Share your good deeds on social media and encourage everyone you know to donate as well – you are the most important tool in ensuring the success of our campaigns.

Help bring a smile to the faces of those who need it most.

The Most Important Facts at a Glance

Why Do I Have to Pay to Download the App?

By downloading our app, you allow us to fund and implement a range of charitable projects. The full amount of your donations goes to the projects you have selected – we use the app download fee to cover our own costs.

How Do I Donate?

At YSCTW, you make donations and support a wide range of charitable projects using just your smile. Simply upload a selfie or any other picture of your choice and select one or several projects for which you would like to make a donation.

How Much Can I Donate, and How Often?

We believe that everyone is equal, and we give everyone equal opportunities to contribute in the same way. You can donate to several projects with every picture you upload, but your donation may not exceed one unit in your chosen currency (US dollars, euros, Swiss francs). Of course, you can still take as many selfies as you want and make as many donations as you like.

How Can I Track My Donation and How Transparent Is the Process?

Your name will be displayed on the list of donors to your chosen project approximately one working day after your donation has been made. Donors are listed in alphabetical order. To ensure full transparency, you can track anybody else’s donation as well as your own at any time.

Can I Donate More per Project?

Yes, by uploading more selfies. We do not accept larger donations, as this goes against the philosophy of our organization: we want to give everyone the same chance to make a difference.

How Much of My Donation Will Actually Go to My Chosen Projects?

The full amount of every donation you make goes directly to the projects you have selected. A small charge will be calculated when you confirm your donation to cover the transaction fees.
